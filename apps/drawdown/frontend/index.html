Drawdown Explorer

Simulate market paths and visualize peak-to-trough drawdowns over time.

Legend

Drawdown
Peak-to-trough % drop from the most recent high. Negative values, e.g., −25%.
P10/P50/P90
Percentiles across simulations at each point in time.
Median max drawdown
Typical worst drop experienced over the whole horizon.
Median recovery
Months from the median path’s trough to regain the prior peak.
